Corrections

Public corrections log

Readers should not have to guess whether a story changed. The site keeps a visible log and individual stories carry their own corrections panels.

Current log

No corrections have been logged in this prototype edition.

When that changes, each entry should include the story title, the date of correction, what changed, and whether the change materially altered the thrust of the piece.

How to request a correction

Email the newsroom with the headline, the disputed claim, and the supporting document or evidence. Use the contact page for the right desk address.

What earns a note

Substantive factual errors, misleading figures, misidentifications, or materially incomplete context should receive a correction or editor’s note.
