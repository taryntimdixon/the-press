Desk rules

  • Cartoons and satire are labeled at the top of the page and on every card.
  • Invented scenes are allowed; invented facts are not presented as reporting.
  • Real public events should get enough context for the joke to be legible.
  • Corrections apply when a cartoon depends on a factual claim that later proves wrong.
