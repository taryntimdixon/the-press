Main idea

Texas Hold’em gets its prime-time moment back

The 2026 WSOP Main Event is returning to ESPN with a three-night live final table, giving poker a rare chance to rebuild a shared, mainstream sports story.

Key points

  • The 2026 WSOP runs May 26–July 15 at Horseshoe and Paris Las Vegas.
  • The $10,000 No-Limit Hold’em Main Event begins July 2.
  • The final table pauses after July 13 and returns August 3–5 for ESPN’s live finale.
  • The 2025 WSOP set series records with 246,960 entrants and more than $481 million awarded.
  • The article treats the ESPN return as a media, trust, and audience-rebuilding story — not merely a schedule item.
