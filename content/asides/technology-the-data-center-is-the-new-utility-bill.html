Key points

  • The article compares public data-center demand projections against EIA 2024 electricity-sales denominators to show scale.
  • EPRI's high 2030 U.S. data-center scenario equals about 19.9% of 2024 retail sales in The Press calculation.
  • A 1 GW load running at 95% utilization uses about 8.3 TWh a year, enough to matter sharply in smaller state systems.
  • The central fight is not only power supply. It is measurement, rate design, local disclosure, water, and who pays if forecasts change.
  • The article uses 110 public source links and source cards, plus original EIA-based calculations stored in the reporting folder.
