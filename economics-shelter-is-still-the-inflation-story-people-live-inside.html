Key points

  • Freddie Mac said the average 30-year mortgage rate was 6.46% on April 2, still high enough to keep buying expensive.
  • NAR reported February existing-home sales at a 4.09 million annual rate with a $398,000 median price and 3.8 months of supply.
  • BLS said shelter rose 0.2% in February and remained the largest factor in the monthly CPI increase, even as rent growth cooled.
  • HUD reported 8.46 million renter households with worst-case housing needs between 2021 and 2023.
