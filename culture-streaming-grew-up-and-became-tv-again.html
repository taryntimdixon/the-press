Key points

  • Nielsen said streaming captured 47.5% of TV viewing in December 2025 and 47.0% in January 2026.
  • Ad-supported streaming accounted for 45.6% of ad-supported TV viewing in Q4 2025.
  • WGA and SAG-AFTRA contracts now include streaming-success bonuses tied to performance thresholds.
  • The Motion Picture Association says the U.S. film and television industry supports 2.01 million jobs and $202 billion in wages.
